BRIEF HISTORY ABOUT THE FAITH ARENA INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

THE FAITH ARENA INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL WAS FOUNDED IN 2006:
In the WESTERN AREA of Freetown Sierra Leone it is located in the west end of the city, at No 2Q COVENNT AVENUE HILL COT ROAD WASH CAR FREETOWN. It was established by the founder/National Superintendent, PASTOR &PASTOR MRS EMMANUAL OLA BODE DANIA it is a Christian school and a mixed school of boys and girls. The school operates as High and Pre-School. The school was established with the proprietor vision to support the Government to arrive in providing standard education in the country while as education is the right to all children and a foundation to economic, social and moral development. As the school is now the first choice of children within the Western Area, it is the proprietor’s dream to establish branches to other Districts in the country as they will also benefit from the standard and good quality education provided by the school which is embedded in its Motto KNOWLEDGE IS WEALTH/PURITY PURPOSE AND PROGRES about us welcome to faith arena international, a center of excellence where learning is purposeful, values are nurtured, and greatness is inspired we proudly cater to pupils from nursery 1 to senior secondary school 3 (sss 3), providing a well-structured and stimulating academic journey tailored to each child’s stage of development. at faith arena international , education goes beyond the classroom. we are committed to raising confident, morally grounded, and intellectually sound individuals who are ready to face the future with courage and character. our team of qualified, passionate, and experienced educators is dedicated to delivering a curriculum that blends academic excellence with creativity, technology, and moral instruction.we offer a safe and nurturing learning environment small class sizes for personalized attention engaging co-curricular activities and clubs A strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, sciences, and life skills A culture of discipline, respect, and excellence Whether you're seeking a solid academic foundation for your young learner or preparing your teenager for higher education and life beyond, [Your School Name] is the place where dreams take root and destinies are shaped, and every Enroll today and be part of a school where learning is living child matters!
